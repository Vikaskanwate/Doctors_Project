About Us

Dr.Shruti Patil (Gynecologist)

Expert Care in Women's Health and Infertility, Compassionately Delivered by Dr. Shruti Patil

Dr. Shruti Patil is a skilled Obstetrician and Gynecologist with 8 years of experience, specializing in advanced infertility treatments. She completed her MBBS and DGO from Seth G. S. Medical College and KEM Hospital, Mumbai. Further enhancing her expertise, she pursued a FOGSI-certified Infertility Fellowship at Gupte Hospital, Pune, and an Ultrasound (USG) course. Dr. Patil is dedicated to providing comprehensive care, including high-risk obstetrics and gynecological services. With her compassionate approach and advanced knowledge, she ensures that every patient receives the highest level of personalized care and support throughout their journey to better health.

Specializations of Dr. Shruti Patil:

Obstetrician

Dr. Shruti Patil offers expert obstetric care, managing normal and high-risk pregnancies, prenatal to postnatal support, ensuring mother and baby’s well-being.

Special Interest in Infertility

Dr. Shruti Patil specializes in infertility treatments, offering ovulation induction, IUI, and ART, with personalized care for optimal success.

Gynecologist

Dr. Shruti Patil provides expert gynecological care, treating menstrual disorders, PCOS, fibroids, and offering routine screenings with personalized care.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in women’s healthcare, offering compassionate, expert care in obstetrics, gynecology, and infertility treatments. We aim to empower women at every stage of life with personalized care, advanced treatments, and unwavering support, ensuring their health and well-being are our top priority.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional, holistic care to women by:
  • Expert Obstetric Care: Offering comprehensive services for both normal and high-risk pregnancies, from prenatal to postnatal care, ensuring the health and safety of both mother and baby.
  • Gynecological Excellence: Providing expert care for a wide range of gynecological concerns, including menstrual disorders, PCOS, fibroids, and routine screenings, with a focus on individualized treatment plans.
  • Infertility Solutions: Specializing in advanced infertility treatments such as ovulation induction, IUI, and ART, with personalized care to maximize success and help couples achieve their dreams of parenthood.
  • Compassionate Approach: Offering a supportive, understanding environment for women, with the goal of creating a relationship of trust and comfort for every patient.
  • Patient-Centered Care: Ensuring that every patient receives tailored treatment, education, and guidance to make informed decisions about their health, empowering them throughout their journey.

Foot & Ankle Care

What is Foot & Ankle Care?

Foot and Ankle Care focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of foot and ankle conditions that affect mobility and overall well-being. The foot and ankle are complex structures comprising bones, muscles, ligaments, and tendons, which play a crucial role in balance, movement, and weight-bearing. Specialized foot and ankle care is essential for treating injuries, deformities, and chronic conditions like arthritis, fractures, and tendonitis. Whether it's a sports injury or a chronic condition, timely care can help restore mobility, relieve pain, and improve quality of life.

Our Foot & Ankle Care Services Include:

  • Ankle Sprain Treatment – Comprehensive care for ankle sprains, including immobilization, rehabilitation, and preventive care to avoid future injuries.
  • Fracture Management – Treatment for foot and ankle fractures, including non-surgical options like casting and surgical intervention if required.
  • Bunion Correction Surgery – Surgical treatment to realign the bones and remove bony protrusions (bunions) that cause pain and discomfort.
  • Heel Pain Treatment (Plantar Fasciitis) – Advanced treatment options to address heel pain caused by inflammation of the plantar fascia ligament.
  • Achilles Tendon Repair – Specialized treatment for Achilles tendon injuries, including ruptures, tendinitis, and tendon tears.
  • Foot and Ankle Arthritis Care – Management of arthritis-related pain and stiffness in the foot and ankle through medication, therapy, and surgery if needed.

Benefits of Foot & Ankle Care:

  • Improved Mobility – Proper care ensures that mobility is restored, allowing patients to return to their daily activities without discomfort.
  • Pain Relief – Effective treatments can alleviate foot and ankle pain, whether caused by injury, arthritis, or other conditions.
  • Preventive Care – Early diagnosis and treatment help prevent complications, such as chronic pain or mobility issues.
  • Faster Recovery from Injuries – Advanced treatment techniques promote faster healing and reduce downtime after foot or ankle injuries.
  • Customized Rehabilitation Plans – Personalized physical therapy and rehabilitation programs help patients regain strength and prevent future injuries.

Conditions We Treat with Foot & Ankle Care:

  • Ankle Sprains and Strains – Common injuries caused by twisting or rolling the ankle, leading to pain and swelling.
  • Foot and Ankle Fractures – Broken bones in the foot or ankle that require immediate attention and sometimes surgical intervention.
  • Plantar Fasciitis – A condition causing heel pain due to inflammation of the plantar fascia ligament.
  • Achilles Tendinitis – Inflammation of the Achilles tendon, often seen in athletes and individuals with high physical activity levels.
  • Bunions – Bony protrusions at the base of the big toe that can cause pain and deformity, often requiring surgical correction.
